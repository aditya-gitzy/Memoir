### 📖 The Story
Ever tried reading your downloaded Instagram chat history? It's kind of a nightmare. You're handed a folder full of backwards HTML files that read like cold server logs. It makes it almost impossible to actually sit down, scroll back, and just reminisce.

**Memoir fixes that.** It takes your messy `message_X.html` files, parses the text, flips them into chronological order, and wraps the whole thing in a delicate, glassmorphism UI. It’s designed to feel less like reading a database and more like flipping through a diary of the time you’ve spent with someone.

### ✨ What Makes It Special
* **🌬️ It Breathes:** Features a soft, frosted-glass design with a dynamic, ambient background and a smooth dark/light mode toggle.
* **🗓️ Paced Reading:** Automatically parses timestamps and injects elegant date-dividers, breaking up endless walls of text into readable "chapters" of days and weeks.
* **🔍 Contextual Search:** Pinpoint that exact inside joke. You can isolate your timeline to only show messages containing specific words, or highlight those words within the full flow of the conversation.
* **🔒 100% Private & Local:** These are your private memories. Memoir has no servers, no databases, and no APIs. Your chat files never leave your computer—everything is parsed and rendered locally right in your browser.

### 🚀 How to Relive Your Memories
1. **Get your data:** Request your download from Instagram (Make sure to select **HTML format**, not JSON).
2. **Find the chat:** Unzip the data, go to your messages, and open the specific chat folder. You'll see files named `message_1.html`, `message_2.html`, etc.
3. **Launch Memoir:** Just open `index.html` in any modern web browser. 
4. **Load them up:** Click "Choose Files", select **all** the HTML files from that folder, and hit format.
5. *Grab a coffee, scroll back to day one, and enjoy.* ☕

### 🛠️ Tech Stack
Built purely with vanilla **HTML**, **CSS**, and **JavaScript**. No frameworks, no dependencies. Just clean code.
